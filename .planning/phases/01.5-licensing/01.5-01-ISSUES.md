### UAT-001: cargo near build fails with bulk memory validation errors

**Discovered:** 2026-01-12
**Phase/Plan:** 01.5-01
**Severity:** Major
**Feature:** WASM build for NEAR deployment
**Resolved:** 2026-01-13 - Fixed in 01.5-01-FIX.md
**Commit:** 5d3cc5f
